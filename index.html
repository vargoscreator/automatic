Light and sturdy for extreme field use

Highly customizable for positional shooting

Uses M-LOK interface for additional options

Fully adjustable stock

Shooter can easily adjust cheek height, cheek position, length of pull, buttstock height and pivot for any rifle scope size and height, type of shooting position, individual features etc.

Stock folds to the right

Folding stock makes storage, transportation and cleaning of the rifle much easier and more comfortable for the shooter. Closed stock fully encloses bolt knob for maximum portability.

New for ARC chassis generation 3.0

New for ARC chassis generation 2.3

Stock fixation in a folded position using a QD-mount, to unfold the stock just pull with your hand.

Automatic ARCA Rail

Is used for attaching your rifle to tripods,bipods and fitting different accessories like ARCA plates, etc.

M-LOK compatible interface

Worldwide accepted M-LOK interface, introduced by Magpul, is now an established standard for accessories attachments. Thus ARC 2.3 chassis’ handguard has 6 M-LOK slots on the bottom and 5+5 slots on both right and left sides.

AR-15 type pistol grip compatible

Shooter can choose from hundreds of AR-15 compatible pistol grips made by third-party manufacturers. We recommend Ergo Grip Deluxe or similar model. Grip should be made without “beavertail”, or slightly modified to eliminate this part.

Integrated steel QD-mount

Four bases for quick detach sling attachment provided for ease of transportation. User can install M-LOK style QD mount on the handguard if needed

Hard stressed parts made from steel

Folding stock lock pin, folding stock rotation shaft, folding stock release button, magazine release and adjustable butt plate are made from steel for added strength

Adjustable magazine height

Chassis for Remington 700 style actions has an adjustable magazine height, to ensure perfect feeding no matter what action and magazine combination you use
